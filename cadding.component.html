What is Cadding?

Computer Aided Design (Cadding) is a digital design which replaces manual documentation. At Eastwood Robotics our perfered cadding softwares are Autodesk Inventor and Autodesk Fusion. We these softwares in order to design our competition robots before the building process. This helps our builders have a better idea of what the design of the robot should look like as well as how it should function. We like to print all of our CADs to place them on our engineering notebook to keep an updated documentation of our robots.

Below we have provided some tutorials of different robots as well other everyday objects. These tutorials will help you understand the mechanics of Autodesk Inventor.
